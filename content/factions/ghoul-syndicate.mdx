# Notice — Ridgeline Camp, Status Change

Ridgeline camp is ours again as of two nights back. Held it four days, lost it to Court forward men for eleven, took it back. Losses: two good workers, one lame but serviceable. The tools left at the camp are accounted for. We did not touch the Court's leavings; they can collect them.

Pending collections for this week: Aldric's consignment (weight: approx. 40 stone rendered, price agreed at nine iron per, total 360 iron) — Aldric knows what he owes and when. Fenwick of the lower Gateward owes carrying fees from last month, now doubled per the standard terms. He has been notified.

New arrangement: the passage below Sallow Lane is now rated at six iron per crossing per party, up from four. The increase is for the water problem, which has not resolved itself and is not going to. Factor accordingly.

One outstanding matter. Someone on or near our patch has been moving goods without telling us. This is not an accusation. It is an observation. The person who knows what this refers to should speak to us before we speak to them.
